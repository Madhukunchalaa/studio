Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 4, 2026. Your trust is our blueprint. We treat your data with the same precision as our pixels.

01. Our Philosophy

At StudioXAI, privacy is not a compliance checklist—it is a core value. We utilize advanced artificial intelligence to create cinematic experiences, and we believe transparency is the foundation of digital creativity.

02. Data We Collect

To provide high-fidelity AI avatar and video services, we collect:

  • Identity Data: Professional contact information (Name, Email, LinkedIn Profile).
  • Biometric Assets: Provided images, video footage, and voice samples for avatar training.
  • Session Data: Technical logs for platform optimization.

03. AI Processing & Security

Personal video and voice data are processed exclusively for the generation of your custom assets. This data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

  • Data is stored in high-security regional cloud environments.
  • Biometric markers are used solely for your projects and are never used to train global, multi-tenant AI models.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your biometric information to any third party.

04. Your Rights & Control

You maintain full ownership of your digital twin and associated data. At any time, you may request:

  • A full audit of the data we hold regarding your projects.
  • Permanent deletion of trained AI weights or source media.
  • Withdrawal of consent for continued AI processing.

05. Ethical Framework

StudioXAI reserves the right to terminate services if provided assets are used for deepfakes, misinformation, or unauthorized impersonation. We only produce content for which you have verified legal rights.
